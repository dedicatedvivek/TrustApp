\begin{abstract}

The path to finding answers to research questions constitutes research process. The various steps involved in a research process are not mutually exclusive nor they are separate and distinct. They do not necessarily follow each other in any specific order and the researcher has to be constantly anticipating at each step in the research process the requirements of the subsequent steps. However the following order concerning various steps provides a useful procedural guideline regarding the research process.
